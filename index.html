About Us

CCBRS is a national coverage local institution, registered in Somaliland and operational since 2003.

CCBRS is working to alleviate the complex barriers encountered by the most marginalized and vulnerable people in Somaliland by providing adequate community based protection, social and development services in equal opportunities in all areas of life. CCBRS has a philosophy of community based and participatory work.

CCBRS improves the lives of marginalized people through community led approaches that are leading to ultimate change

Vission

CCBRS is seen as local professional organization working to alleviate the complex barriers encountered by the most marginalized and vulnerable people in Somaliland by providing adequate community based protection, social and development services in equal opportunities in all areas of life.

Mission

To improve the quality of the disadvantaged and vulnerable groups of its target communities in Somaliland with a view of reaching their full protection, social and economic integration and participation of equal rights and access to comprehensive and equitable opportunities regardless of gender, racial, colour, cause and religious issues.

What We Do

Comprehensive Community Based Rehabilitation in Somaliland CCBRS mainly focuses on the below areas.

Protection

  1. Empowering Community Based Child Protection Committees and Systems to Prevent and Response to Child Rights Violation Program in Somali-land Project
  2. Community FGM abandonment through women empowerment and drought mitigation Project

Food Security and Livelihood

  1. Developing a sustainable, climate-adjusted approach to food security and increasing resilience project in the Maroodi Jeeh region of Somali-land Project
  2. Self-help group project for vulnerable women and girls in Hargeisa IDP settlements Project

Emergency response shelter

  1. Distribution of food, shelter materials, Non-food items (NFIs) and dignity kits for drought effected households and poor women and girls project
  2. Provision of NFIs, dignity kits and solar lamps to new IDPs in Togdheer Region

Health

  1. Provision of quality and comprehensive primary healthcare assistance to vulnerable Yemeni refugee and asylum-seeker population Project
  2. Rehabilitation and protection of persons with disabilities and people with mental health problems Project

Water

  1. Promotion water hygiene and sanitation through constructing of water sites, latrines and disposal points in IDP camps
  2. Rehabilitation and protection of persons with disabilities and people with mental health problems

Nutrition

  1. Emergency food and water distribution for drought effected household
  2. Provision of quality and comprehensive primary healthcare assistance to vulnerable Yemeni refugee and asylum-seeker population Project
